1.0 OBJECTIVE:

The objective of the Quality review of product is the formal verification of the consistency of the process. It is aimed at:

  • Detecting any untoward trend pattern or bias and addressing them on scientific and logical basis to maintain the pre-specified identity, quality, purity of the API’s.
  • To assure that the product review provides adequate and reproducible assurance that the specific process consistently performs to produce product meeting its pre-determined specification and quality characteristics.
  • The attributes of the APIs are under predetermined limits as stipulated in the VMP, Process and Product are in “validated state”.

2.0 SCOPE:

This product review encompasses the entire synthesis, purification of product manufactured at Rau site of Symbiotec Pharmalab Pvt. Ltd. Indore.

The product quality review is a formal, official and mandatory process of reviewing the API product to have a panoramic view of the performance of all the manufacturing, controlling and monitoring procedures employed to manufacturing the product at Symbiotec Pharmalab Pvt. Ltd. Rau, Indore site.

The results of this review are to be evaluated and an assessment is to be made of whether any corrective action or any revalidation should be undertaken. The corrective actions are documented and the agreed corrective actions, completion timelines are described as well.
